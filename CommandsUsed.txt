Commands used in making of Nextjs app to connect deployed smart contract from Ganache

1) To deploy contarct in ganache
	1.1] npm i truffle
	1.2] truffle init
	1.3] truffle compile
	1.4] truffle migrate --network development
   Deployed in Ganache 
   
2) To create nextjs app 
	2.1] npm install next@latest react@latest react-dom@latest
